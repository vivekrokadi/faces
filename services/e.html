Our professional eye makeup typically lasts 8-12 hours. For special occasions, we offer long-wear formulations that can last up to 16 hours. We also provide touch-up kits and tips to extend the wear of your eye makeup.

Threading is generally superior to waxing for several reasons: it's more precise, removes finer hairs, causes less skin irritation, and doesn't involve heat or chemicals.
