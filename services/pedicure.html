Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I get a manicure or pedicure?

We recommend a manicure or pedicure every 3-4 weeks for healthy nails and soft skin.

What are the benefits of a pedicure?

Pedicures help remove dead skin, improve circulation, prevent nail infections, and keep feet soft and healthy.

Is a French manicure long-lasting?

Yes! A French manicure lasts 1-2 weeks with regular polish and up to 3 weeks with gel polish.

Do you offer nail treatments for brittle nails?

Yes, we offer nail strengthening treatments that help prevent breakage and promote healthier nail growth.
