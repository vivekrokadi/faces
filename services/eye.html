Frequently Asked Questions

How long does eye makeup last?

Eye makeup can last 8-12 hours with high-quality products and proper setting techniques.

Is threading better than waxing for eyebrows?

Yes! Threading is more precise and gentle on sensitive skin compared to waxing. It gives better shape and lasts longer.

How long do eyelash extensions last?

Eyelash extensions typically last 4-6 weeks, depending on natural lash growth and aftercare.

Can I get eye makeup done for sensitive eyes?

Yes! We use hypoallergenic and waterproof makeup to ensure comfort and safety for sensitive eyes.
