Frequently Asked Questions

How long do nail extensions last?

Nail extensions typically last 3-4 weeks, depending on your daily activities and nail care routine.

Can I get custom nail art?

Yes! We offer customized nail designs based on your preferences, including bridal nail art, 3D designs, and trendy styles.

Are nail extensions safe for natural nails?

Yes, when applied and removed correctly by professionals, nail extensions do not harm your natural nails. We also offer nail strengthening treatments.

What’s the difference between gel polish and regular polish?

Gel polish lasts 2-3 weeks without chipping and gives a glossy finish, while regular polish lasts about a week but is easier to remove.
